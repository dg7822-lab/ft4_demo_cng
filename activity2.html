Lack of Freshwater in Coastal Villages

Problem: Many coastal villages face severe freshwater shortages because available water is salty or brackish.

Simple Idea: Use wave energy to power a small desalination unit that converts seawater into drinkable water using reverse osmosis.

Explanation: The system uses wave motion to drive pumps that generate pressure for desalination. This produces clean, drinkable water for communities using renewable energy.
